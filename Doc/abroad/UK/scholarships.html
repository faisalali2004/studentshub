Student Hub

  • University of Oxford

    The Clarendon Fund offers full and partial scholarships to graduate students from around the world, while the Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies Scholarships supports graduate students who are studying Islamic studies.

  • University of Cambridge

    The Gates Cambridge Scholarship is a highly competitive scholarship that provides full funding for graduate study at the University of Cambridge. The university also offers a variety of other scholarships and bursaries to international students.

  • Imperial College London

    Imperial College Business School offers scholarships to international students pursuing MBA and Masters programs. Imperial also offers a variety of other scholarships for international students across all degree levels.

  • University of Edinburgh

    The University of Edinburgh offers the Edinburgh Global Research Scholarship to international students who are pursuing a PhD program. The university also offers a variety of other scholarships and bursaries to international students.

  • University of Bristol

    The University of Bristol offers the Think Big Scholarship to international undergraduate and postgraduate students. The scholarship covers a portion of tuition fees and living costs.

  • University of Warwick

    The University of Warwick offers a variety of scholarships to international students at all degree levels, including the Chancellor's Scholarship and the Warwick Merit Scholarship.

  • University of Manchester

    The University of Manchester offers the Manchester Global Scholarship to international undergraduate and postgraduate students. The scholarship covers a portion of tuition fees and living costs.
