Top Universities in America

  • Brigham Young University

    Located in Provo, Utah, Brigham Young University (BYU) is a private research university affiliated with The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It is known for its strong programs in business, engineering, and law, among others.

  • University of Dayton

    The University of Dayton is a private Catholic research university located in Dayton, Ohio. It offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, including business, engineering, law, and education.

  • Louisiana State University

    Louisiana State University (LSU) is a public research university located in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. It is the flagship institution of the Louisiana State University System and is known for its strong programs in engineering, business, and mass communication.

  • The University of Tulsa

    The University of Tulsa is a private research university located in Tulsa, Oklahoma. It offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, including engineering, law, and business.

  • University of Alabama at Birmingham

    The University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB) is a public research university located in Birmingham, Alabama. It is known for its strong programs in health and medical sciences, as well as business and engineering.

  • Arizona State University

    Arizona State University (ASU) is a public research university located in the Phoenix metropolitan area. It is known for its innovative approach to higher education and offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in areas such as engineering, business, and journalism.

  • Auburn University

    Auburn University is a public research university located in Auburn, Alabama. It is known for its strong programs in engineering, business, and agriculture, among others.

  • Oregon State University

    Oregon State University (OSU) is a public research university located in Corvallis, Oregon. It offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in areas such as engineering, forestry, and public health.

  • University of Arizona

    The University of Arizona is apublic research university located in Tucson, Arizona. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in areas such as business, engineering, and medicine. The university is also known for its strong programs in astronomy and planetary science.

  • University of California, Berkeley

    The University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley) is a public research university located in Berkeley, California. It is known for its strong programs in science, engineering, and economics, among others. UC Berkeley is also home to many world-renowned research institutes and centers.
