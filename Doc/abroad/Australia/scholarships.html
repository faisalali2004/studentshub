Australian Scholarships

  • Australia Awards

    This scholarship program is funded by the Australian government and is open to students from selected countries in the Asia-Pacific, Middle East, and Africa regions. The scholarship covers tuition fees, travel expenses, and living allowances.

  • Endeavour Postgraduate Scholarship Awards

    This scholarship program is also funded by the Australian government and is open to international students who want to undertake postgraduate study in Australia. The scholarship covers tuition fees, travel expenses, and a living allowance.

  • International Postgraduate Research Scholarships (IPRS)

    This scholarship program is open to international students who want to undertake a research degree at an Australian university. The scholarship covers tuition fees, health cover costs, and a living allowance.

  • University-specific scholarships

    Many Australian universities offer scholarships to international students. These scholarships may be based on academic merit, financial need, or other criteria. You can check the websites of individual universities for more information.

  • Australia APEC Women in Research Fellowship

    This scholarship is funded by the Australian government and is open to female researchers from APEC economies to undertake research in partnership with Australian universities.

  • Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarships

    This scholarship program is open to domestic and international students who want to undertake a research degree (Masters or PhD) at an Australian university. The scholarship covers tuition fees, health cover costs, and a living allowance.

  • International Undergraduate Scholarships

    Many Australian universities offer scholarships to international students who want to undertake an undergraduate degree. These scholarships may be based on academic merit, financial need, or other criteria.

  • Destination Australia Scholarship

    This scholarship program is funded by the Australian government and is open to domestic and international students who want to study in regional Australia. The scholarship covers tuition fees and a living allowance.
