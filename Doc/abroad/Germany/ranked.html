Top Universities in Germany

  • Technische Universität München (TUM)

    Located in Munich, TUM is the first university in Germany to be named a University of Excellence by the German government. It is particularly strong in engineering, natural sciences, and medicine.

  • Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München (LMU)

    Also located in Munich, LMU is one of the oldest universities in Germany and has a strong reputation in the humanities and social sciences.

  • Ruprecht-Karls-Universität Heidelberg

    Located in Heidelberg, this university has a long history dating back to the 14th century and is particularly strong in medicine, natural sciences, and humanities.

  • Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin

    Located in the heart of Berlin, this university was founded in 1810 and has a strong reputation in the humanities and social sciences.

  • Freie Universität Berlin

    Another top university in Berlin, Freie Universität Berlin was founded in 1948 and is particularly strong in the social sciences and humanities.

  • Technische Universität Berlin

    With a strong focus on engineering and technology, this university is located in the center of Berlin.

  • Karlsruher Institut für Technologie (KIT)

    This university was formed by a merger between the University of Karlsruhe and the Karlsruhe Research Center in 2009 and is particularly strong in engineering and natural sciences.

  • Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen

    Located in Tübingen, this university is particularly strong in the humanities and social sciences, as well as medicine.

  • Universität Hamburg

    One of the largest universities in Germany, Universität Hamburg is located in the heart of the city and has a strong reputation in the natural sciences and humanities.

  • Universität Freiburg

    Located in the city of Freiburg, this university is known for its strong programs in the humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences.

  • Technische Universität Dresden

    Located in the city of Dresden, this university is particularly strong in engineering and technology.

  • Universität Stuttgart

    This university is located in the city of Stuttgart and is particularly strong in engineering, natural sciences, and mathematics.

  • Universität Mannheim

    Located in Mannheim, this university is particularly strong in the social sciences and economics.

  • Johann Wolfgang Goethe-Universität Frankfurt am Main

    Located in the city of Frankfurt, this university is particularly strong in the humanities, social sciences, and economics.

  • Universität zu Köln

    This university is located in the city of Cologne and is particularly strong in the humanities and social sciences.

  • Universität Münster

    Located in the city of Münster, this university is particularly strong in the natural sciences, medicine, and humanities.

  • Universität Duisburg-Essen

    This university was formed by a merger between the University of Duisburg and the University of Essen in 2003 and is particularly strong in the natural sciences, engineering, and economics.

  • Technische Universität Darmstadt

    Located in the city of Darmstadt, this university is particularly strong in engineering and technology.

  • Universität Leipzig

    Founded in 1409, this university is located in the city of Leipzig and is particularly strong in the humanities and social sciences.

  • Universität Bayreuth

    This university is located in the city of Bayreuth and is particularly strong in the natural sciences, engineering, and economics, with a focus on interdisciplinary research.

  • Universität Konstanz

    Located on the shores of Lake Constance, this university is particularly strong in the social sciences, natural sciences, and humanities, and has a focus on international and interdisciplinary research.

  • Technische Universität Braunschweig

    This university is located in the city of Braunschweig and is particularly strong in engineering and technology, with a focus on applied research and innovation.

  • Universität Bremen

    Located in the city of Bremen, this university is particularly strong in the natural sciences, social sciences, and humanities, with a focus on interdisciplinary research.

  • Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg

    Located in the cities of Erlangen and Nuremberg, this university is particularly strong in the natural sciences, engineering, and medicine, and has a focus on interdisciplinary research and innovation.
